our values and mission statement

We are an organisation that believes that all animals should be treated with respect, kindness, and love. Animals are our friends and not a commodity or food. Our mission is to end all exploitation and cruelty of animals in farms, laboratories, and the home.

Our Work

We work all over the world to end animal cruelty and exploitation on all levels and in all countries. We will not rest until every animal is liberated!
